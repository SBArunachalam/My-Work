The Creation Process

From a conversation to a piece that hangs on your wall — here's the journey

💬
01

Reach out via WhatsApp. Share your vision, preferred size, colours, and the feeling you want the piece to carry.

✏️
02
Sketch & Plan

A rough blueprint is created — ring geometry, petal count, and symmetry are all planned before a single stroke hits paper.

Layer by layer, working outward from the seed centre — each ring drawn freehand with precision pens or brushes.

✦ The Artist

Drawing Circles Since Childhood

The Dharanika Artverse was born from a single midnight session — a circle drawn on notebook paper that never quite closed, then another, and another. What began as meditation became craft, craft became calling.

Every mandala is a map of stillness. I work in meditative silence, beginning always at the seed centre and moving outward — the way consciousness itself unfolds. No two pieces share the same geometry. Each is drawn in a single devoted session, usually lasting 8 to 20 hours.

The studio ships across India. I work with individuals, families, interior designers, yoga studios, and anyone who believes that walls should speak.
